Foundation

What Exactly Is the Stock Market?

The stock market is where people buy small ownership shares in real companies such as Apple, Tesla, or Dangote.

When those companies grow in value, your investment grows with them.

Instead of leaving money idle in a savings account, investing allows your capital to work for you and compound over time. Disciplined investors build wealth gradually over 5 to 10 years.

It is not gambling
It is not only for the wealthy
It is a structured financial system once understood
